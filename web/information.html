About this proxy

Public HTTP/SOCKS5 outproxy. *.onion goes through Tor, *.i2p through i2pd, everything else goes out directly.

How to use

  • SOCKS5: socks5h://THIS_HOST:1080 (use socks5h so the proxy resolves names)
  • HTTP/HTTPS: http://THIS_HOST:3128
